About Me

Hi my name is Jennie, I have had the pleasure of sharing in the joy of countless special wedding days by providing my bridal hair and makeup service. I have a knack for understanding your vision and bringing it to life. I studied at Paul Mitchell The School Jersey Shore, and while I was there I learned the techniques that make for the most elegant updos for brides and their bridesmaids. I love working with all different kinds of braids and twists and look forward to sharing in more unforgettable moments.
